  • For Schools
    Aarohan is a one of a kind nationwide school level event, organised by BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus. The event will consist of a regional level round, conducted online in multiple schools across the country. The school gets to analyze their students' ability on a national level, and can get to know the specific strengths and weaknesses of the students as compared to peers across the country. The school also gets recognition on the national level of their students' performance.
  • For Students
    Aarohan is a one of a kind nationwide school level event , organised by the BITS Pilani , Pilani Campus. The event will consist of a regional level round , conducted online in multiple schools across the country. Students will get a chance to test their knowledge , logical aptitude and coding skills against other students from their region and compete for top honours.

  • Braniac

    Think outside the box !

    Braniac , is a test designed to test the application of simple scientific concepts that you all learn in your classes, and the logical aptitude of the students.

    The venue of the exams would be your school. The questions would be objective in nature and the test will be OMR based. The duration of the exam would be 60 minutes.The test paper will consist of:
    Section A: Basic sciences and mathematics 30 questions
    Section B: Logical aptitude and mental reasoning 15 questions

    In order to be eligible to take the exam the student needs to register for it through the school and pay the required registration fee. Each student, once registered, will be provided with a login id and password, that will be sent to their e-mail address. After logging on to the Aarohan website, you'll be able to access your admit card, which you'll require on the day of the exam. Results of Braniac will be out by the end of January.

  • Code Voyage

    A test of aptitude and skill !

    The purpose of the exam is to test aptitude for coding, ability to think clearly and in a concise manner and knowledge of various coding languages.The exam would be held simultaneously within one city on a single day and would be computer based.

    In order to be eligible to take the exam the student needs to register for it through the school and pay the required registration fee. Each student, once registered, will be provided with a login id and password, to login into the Aarohan website. After logging in, each participant will be given a link to the CodeChef website, which will take them to their exam window. Participants are supposed to take the test as per scheduled mentioned on the CodeChef website. This exam can be taken from the comfort of your homes.The exam would contain a problem whose solution the student needs to come up in the form of a computer code.

    Students can use the following languages: C, C++, JAVA, Python.The duration of the exam would be 60 minutes.The test paper will consist of 5 questions.There will be certain star marked questions which will be used as tie breakers.

    Results of Code Voyage will be out by the end of January.
